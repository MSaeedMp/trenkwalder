# Benefits

## Health Insurance

All full-time employees are eligible for comprehensive health insurance starting from day one. Coverage includes medical, dental, and vision plans. Family members can be added during the annual enrollment period in November.

The company covers 80% of the premium for individual plans and 70% for family plans. Employees can choose between three tiers: Basic (general practitioner visits and emergency care), Standard (includes specialist visits and mental health support), and Premium (adds private hospital rooms and international coverage).

Preventive care — annual checkups, vaccinations, and screenings — is fully covered under all tiers at no additional cost.

## Time Off

### Paid Leave

Employees receive 25 days of paid vacation per year. Unused days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Any days beyond the carry-over limit expire on March 31st. Public holidays are observed in addition to vacation days.

Vacation requests should be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal. During peak periods (July, August, December), early submission is strongly recommended. Managers may ask employees to adjust dates if too many team members request the same period.

### Parental Leave

New parents are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave at full salary. This applies equally to all parents regardless of gender. Leave can be taken flexibly within the first 12 months after birth or adoption.

Parents returning from leave can request a gradual return — working 60%, 80%, or 100% of their regular hours for the first three months. The company guarantees the same role or an equivalent position upon return.

### Sick Leave

Employees must notify their manager by 9:00 AM on the first day of illness. A medical certificate is required for absences longer than three consecutive working days. The company provides full salary continuation for up to six weeks per calendar year.

## Retirement

The company matches employee contributions to the retirement plan up to 5% of gross salary. Vesting is immediate — there is no waiting period. Employees can choose between conservative, balanced, and growth investment options.

Contributions are deducted automatically from monthly salary. Employees can adjust their contribution rate at any time through the HR portal. Financial planning consultations are available once per year at no cost.

## Perks

Employees enjoy a monthly public transport subsidy of €50, a home office equipment allowance of €500 (one-time), and access to the company gym at headquarters. Team lunches are provided every Friday.

Additional perks include 10% discount at partner restaurants near the Vienna office, two company-wide social events per year (summer party and holiday celebration), and a referral bonus of €1,500 for each successful hire through employee referrals.
